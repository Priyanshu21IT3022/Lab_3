Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato

Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over medium heat. 2. Assemble the sandwich with bread, cheese, bacon, and tomato. 3. Place the sandwich in the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted. 4. Serve and enjoy!
